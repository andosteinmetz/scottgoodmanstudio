Scott Goodman is a painter and sculptor living in Brooklyn, NY. He received a BFA from Cooper Union (2006) and has collaborated with the art collective DADDY. Goodman's work explores the synthetic relationship of nature unto the domestic, and by extension, issues of perception and reality.
